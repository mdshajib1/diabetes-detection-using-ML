Diabetes Management Tips

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid sugary drinks and processed foods.

2. Regular Exercise

Engage in regular physical activity to help control your blood sugar levels and improve your overall health.

3. Monitor Blood Sugar Levels

Regularly check your blood sugar levels to manage and understand how different foods and activities affect them.

4. Take Medications as Prescribed

Follow your healthcare provider's instructions regarding medications and insulin therapy.

5. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and help your body function properly.

6. Get Regular Check-ups

Schedule regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor your diabetes and overall health.
